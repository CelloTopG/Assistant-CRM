# WCFCB Assistant CRM Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **WCFCB Assistant CRM** – an omnichannel AI assistant for the Workers' Compensation Fund Control Board (WCFCB) of Zambia, built on the Frappe/ERPNext platform.

WCFCB Assistant CRM brings AI-powered support, real-time CoreBusiness data, and a unified inbox together across web and social channels.

## What is WCFCB Assistant CRM?

WCFCB Assistant CRM is a production-ready Frappe app that:

- Provides AI assistants **Anna** (customer support) and **Antoine** (reports & analytics)
- Connects web chat, WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, email, USSD and other channels into a **single unified inbox**
- Integrates with WCFCB CoreBusiness systems for **live claims, payments, employer and beneficiary data**
- Offers dashboards, reports and monitoring tailored to WCFCB operations

## What's Included

The app ships with:

- **2+ AI assistants** for support, reporting and analytics
- **8+ communication channels** via web, social media, messaging and USSD
- **35+ DocTypes and reports** for conversations, claims, employers, satisfaction and performance
- **Omnichannel router & webhooks** via direct platform integrations (WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, etc.)
- **Live data services** for CoreBusiness and ERPNext
- **Security, auditing and monitoring** for production use

For a detailed technical view, see the [System Architecture](technical/architecture.md) guide.

## Who is this app for?

WCFCB Assistant CRM is designed for:

- **Contact center agents** who respond to citizen and employer queries across channels
- **WCFCB operations teams** who monitor service levels, claims and payments
- **IT & integration teams** who manage CoreBusiness, APIs and infrastructure
- **Leadership & analysts** who rely on consolidated reports and dashboards
